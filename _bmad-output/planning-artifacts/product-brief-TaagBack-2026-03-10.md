# Product Brief: TaagBack

## Executive Summary

TaagBack is a QR-code overlay platform that transforms the world's billions of existing QR codes into an interactive game layer. Using the TaagBack mobile app as a "magic lens," users discover a hidden world layered on top of everyday QR codes — scanning, claiming, naming, and collecting "Taags" while competing on leaderboards and progressing through creator-built scavenger hunts. Unlike every existing QR platform that generates new codes, TaagBack repurposes the physical world's existing QR infrastructure as a pre-built game board, turning mundane scanning into discovery, competition, and adventure.

The platform rests on three MVP pillars — a Hunt Builder for sequencing real-world QR codes into clue-driven scavenger hunts, a Taag Leaderboard & Databank for competitive sourcing and claiming, and a Re-scan Retention Mechanic that keeps users physically returning to maintain their claims. These three pillars form a self-reinforcing flywheel: discovery feeds creation, creation feeds play, play feeds discovery — and every interaction quietly builds TaagBack's most valuable strategic asset: a crowdsourced global QR code registry.

TaagBack enters a validated white space in the $6.18B location-based entertainment market with zero direct competitors offering QR-code-overlay scavenger hunts with a creator economy model.

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Billions of QR codes are deployed across the physical world — on storefronts, restaurant tables, museum exhibits, transit stops, event booths, and product packaging. Every one of them is a dumb, one-dimensional link. They redirect to a URL and nothing more. Meanwhile, people crave real-world interactive experiences — exploration, collection, competition, discovery — but existing solutions like geocaching require dedicated infrastructure, specialized apps, and high setup costs. There is no platform that treats the world's existing QR codes as a ready-made interactive canvas.

### Problem Impact

- **For players/explorers:** No accessible way to turn everyday surroundings into an adventure. Geocaching has a steep learning curve and niche audience. Location-based games require custom infrastructure. The real world is full of scannable touchpoints that do nothing interesting.
- **For creators (educators, tourism boards, event organizers, businesses):** Building interactive real-world experiences requires printing custom materials, purchasing specialized platforms, or hiring scavenger hunt vendors. The QR codes they need are often already in place — but no tool lets them leverage what exists.
- **For the market:** QR code adoption is universal post-COVID, yet the QR ecosystem remains fragmented — millions of codes pointing to dead links, outdated campaigns, or defunct businesses. No one is curating, cataloging, or adding value to this massive physical-world infrastructure.

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

- **Geocaching** requires intent, a dedicated app, and physical cache placement — high barrier for both creators and casual players
- **QR code platforms** (Beaconstac, QR Code Generator, Flowcode) focus exclusively on *generating new codes* with analytics — none repurpose existing ones
- **Scavenger hunt apps** (GooseChase, Scavify) are event-focused tools, not persistent platforms — they require custom setup for every experience and don't build cumulative value
- **Location-based games** (Pokémon GO, Ingress) use augmented reality and proprietary POI databases — massive infrastructure investment with no creator economy for everyday users
- **No platform treats existing QR codes as claimable, collectible, game-enabled touchpoints**

### Proposed Solution

TaagBack is a mobile-first platform where the app acts as a magic lens revealing a hidden game layer on any QR code. The core experience spans three interconnected loops:

1. **Discover & Collect** — Scan any real-world QR code to "source" it as a Taag. First scanners earn permanent Pioneer credit and naming rights. Build a personal collection of every code you've encountered.
2. **Compete & Maintain** — Claim Taags for leaderboard status. Maintain claims through monthly re-scans — miss a month and your Taag becomes contestable. Watchlist coveted Taags and snipe expired claims.
3. **Create & Play** — Build scavenger hunts by sequencing existing Taags with clues. Share hunts via deep links. Players follow clues through the real world, scanning codes to progress, culminating in TaagBack's signature "blackout crescendo" completion moment.

Every user interaction feeds the platform's flywheel and builds the crowdsourced Taag databank — the hidden strategic asset beneath the game.

### Key Differentiators

- **QR Overlay Model** — The only platform that repurposes existing real-world QR codes rather than generating new ones. The world's billions of deployed codes become TaagBack's pre-built game board.
- **The Flywheel** — Three self-reinforcing pillars (Hunt Builder, Taag Databank, Re-scan Retention) where each pillar feeds the others, creating compounding network effects.
- **Native App as Competitive Moat** — TaagBack intercepts QR codes at the decoder level, reading raw encoded data before URL resolution. This technical requirement is the moat — competitors can't replicate the overlay without controlling the scan.
- **Crowdsourced Data Asset** — Users play a game; TaagBack builds the world's most comprehensive registry of physical QR code deployments — valuable to retail analytics, city planners, and marketing agencies.
- **"Protect the Magic" Design Philosophy** — Every feature decision prioritizes wonder, discovery, and personal ownership over metrics and social transparency. Combined with "Low Floor, High Ceiling," this ensures instant delight for newcomers with bottomless depth for power users.
- **Zero Direct Competitors** — Market research confirms no existing platform combines QR-code overlay, scavenger hunts, and a creator economy model.

## Target Users

### Primary Users

**The Adventurer — "Jake, 22, College Student"**
Active seeker who downloads TaagBack intentionally. Think geocaching enthusiast energy — loves the thrill of the hunt, competition, and collection. Jake sees TaagBack as a new game to master. He sources Taags aggressively, climbs leaderboards, claims territory, and defends it with monthly re-scans. He's the power user who builds the Taag databank through sheer volume. Discovery → Competition → Status is his loop.

**The Wanderer — "Mia, 14, High Schooler"**
Casual discoverer who encounters a TaagBack QR code in the wild — maybe on a coffee shop window or a school bulletin board. Scans it out of curiosity with the TaagBack app, gets the pioneer celebration, names her first Taag, and is hooked. She didn't come looking for TaagBack; TaagBack found her. Serendipity → Delight → Collection is her loop. She's the viral growth engine — every shared moment pulls in more Wanderers.

**The Creator — "Sarah, 35, Teacher / Local Enthusiast"**
Starts as a player, graduates into creation. Sarah played a few hunts, sourced a bunch of Taags, and now wants to build experiences for others — a campus scavenger hunt for her students, a downtown tour for visitors. She uses both the draft-as-you-go field builder and the map-based couch builder. The crowdsourced Taag databank is her creative palette. Play → Inspiration → Creation is her loop.

### Secondary Users

**Business/Organization Users (Post-MVP)**
Tourism boards, event organizers, corporate team building coordinators, and educators who adopt TaagBack as a tool for structured experiences. They represent the B2B monetization tier but are NOT the MVP audience — the platform needs a healthy Taag databank and player base before business use cases have value. They arrive when the flywheel is spinning.

### User Journey

**The Wanderer's First Experience (Signature Journey):**
1. **Discovery** — Encounters a QR code in the real world. Scans with TaagBack app (or installs via deep link from a friend's shared moment).
2. **Onboarding** — First scan triggers pioneer celebration with confetti and fanfare. "You're the FIRST to bring this Taag into TaagBack!" Immediate dopamine hit.
3. **Investment** — Names the Taag (AI suggests contextual options). Learns about monthly re-scan to keep the claim. Retention hook delivered at peak emotional investment.
4. **Core Loop** — Scans more codes. Some are unclaimed (celebration!), some are already claimed ("darn!" moment — but still added to collection). Collection grows. Leaderboard position climbs.
5. **Aha! Moment** — Scans a Taag that's part of an active hunt. Gets invited in. Follows clues through the real world. Completes the hunt with TaagBack's signature blackout crescendo moment.
6. **Long-term** — Becomes a regular sourcer/collector. Watchlists contested Taags. Eventually creates their own hunt from Taags they've sourced. Player becomes Creator.

**The Adventurer's Journey:**
1. **Discovery** — Hears about TaagBack through word of mouth or social media. Downloads intentionally.
2. **Core Loop** — Systematically sources Taags across neighborhoods. Competes on leaderboards. Defends claims with monthly re-scans.
3. **Depth** — Pursues category badges ("Foodie Explorer," "Digital Archaeologist"). Hunts for rare Ghost Taags at defunct businesses. Becomes a local legend.

## Success Metrics

**North Star Metric:** Steady month-over-month growth in active users and platform interactions across the first 12 months post-launch.

### User Success Metrics

| Metric | What It Measures | Target |
|--------|-----------------|--------|
| **Monthly Active Scanners** | Users who scan at least 1 QR code per month | Steady MoM increase |
| **Taags Sourced (cumulative)** | Total unique QR codes brought into the platform | Growing databank = growing value for all users |
| **Re-scan Retention Rate** | % of claimers who return monthly to maintain claims | >40% indicates the retention mechanic works |
| **Hunt Completion Rate** | % of players who start a hunt and finish it | >50% indicates hunts are engaging and well-paced |
| **Player-to-Creator Conversion** | % of players who create at least one hunt | Any measurable conversion validates the flywheel |

### Business Objectives

| Timeframe | Objective |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Month 1-3** | Prove the core loop — users scan, claim, and return to re-scan. Hunts get created and completed. No revenue focus. |
| **Month 4-6** | Validate the flywheel — new Taags feed hunt creation, hunts attract new players, players source more Taags. Organic growth without paid acquisition. |
| **Month 7-12** | Sustained engagement — returning users outnumber churned users each month. Databank grows consistently. Creator community emerges organically. Begin evaluating monetization readiness. |

### Key Performance Indicators

**Growth (the core signal):**
- Monthly Active Users (MAU) — increasing each month
- New user registrations per month — steady or accelerating
- Taags sourced per month — growing (proves real-world engagement)

**Engagement (the loop is working):**
- Scans per active user per month — stable or increasing
- Hunts created per month — growing as player-to-creator pipeline matures
- Average hunts completed per player — indicates content quality and discovery

**Retention (the flywheel holds):**
- Month-1 retention rate (% of new users active 30 days later)
- Re-scan maintenance rate (% of claimed Taags defended monthly)
- Week-over-week active user trend — no sustained downward weeks

**Databank Health (the strategic asset):**
- Total unique Taags in the system
- Geographic spread of Taags (number of distinct neighborhoods/cities)
- Taag contestation rate (% of Taags that change hands) — healthy churn means the game is alive

## MVP Scope

### Core Features

**Pillar 1: Taag Sourcing & Collection**
- QR code scanning via native app (intercepts raw encoded data before URL resolution)
- Taag sourcing — any real-world QR code becomes a Taag in the system
- First Claimer / Pioneer system with permanent Original Discoverer credit
- Custom naming for claimed Taags with content moderation
- Three-tier attribution: Original Discoverer, Current Controller, Custom Name
- Taag collection tracking — every scan adds to your personal collection
- Basic Taag profile cards

**Pillar 2: Hunt Builder & Play**
- Hunt creation: draft-as-you-go (in-field) AND map-based builder (from couch)
- Clue sequencing and progression — clue → solve → walk → scan → reward → next
- Hunt completion with dramatic blackout crescendo celebration
- Creator's custom completion message
- Deep link sharing for hunts (no printing required)
- Hunt discovery through organic Taag scans (hunts find players)

**Pillar 3: Retention & Competition**
- Monthly re-scan to maintain Taag claims
- Watchlist for contested Taags with notifications
- Leaderboards: sourcing, scanning, hunt completion
- Basic player profiles with stats

**Supporting Features:**
- Safety reminders and contextual TOU gate before location-based activities
- Community reporting system (flag any Taag or hunt)
- User authentication (anonymous-first with account promotion)

### Out of Scope for MVP

**Near-MVP (Early Post-Launch Additions):**
- Gen AI Taag name suggestions and clue assistant
- Auto-categorization of Taag types from URL analysis (Restaurant, Ghost, etc.)
- Category-based badges and titles
- Ghost Taag special visual treatment
- Location drift detection and duplicate code detection
- Taag expiration for unscanned codes
- Hunt reviews and ratings
- Local activity feed
- Social sharing at peak moments
- QR encoded URL background analytics
- PWA web entry point

**Future Vision:**
- Auto-generated / AI-assembled experiences
- Challenge Gyms (PvP territory at designated locations)
- Capture the Flag mode
- Taag enrichment with photos/video at sourcing
- Passive hunt creation from scan patterns
- Cosmetic marketplace, Sponsored Taags, B2B tier
- Data insights monetization
- Pioneer Events for new city expansion
- Context-aware dynamic QR content

### MVP Success Criteria

The MVP is validated when the following signals are observed:
- **The core loop works** — users scan, claim, name, and return to re-scan without prompting
- **Hunts get created AND completed** — both sides of the creator/player equation function
- **Organic return behavior** — users come back without push notifications or marketing nudges
- **Databank grows** — new Taags are sourced consistently week over week
- **No single-session churn** — users who complete their first scan return for a second session

**Go/No-Go for Post-MVP investment:** Positive month-over-month growth in MAU and Taags sourced for 3 consecutive months.

### Future Vision

At full maturity, TaagBack becomes the world's QR code overlay platform — a hidden interactive layer on top of physical reality. The crowdsourced Taag databank evolves into the most comprehensive global registry of physical QR code deployments. The creator economy expands from individual hunt builders to tourism boards, educators, and enterprises. Monetization flows through freemium creator tools, a B2B organization tier, a cosmetic marketplace, and sponsored Taags — all passing the "protect the magic" test. The platform's data intelligence (anonymized, aggregated foot traffic and engagement patterns) becomes a standalone commercial asset. Every QR code in the world is a potential TaagBack touchpoint — and every scan makes the platform smarter.
